I am a second-year M.S. student at SWUFE, Chengdu, China. My research focuses on binaural audio synthesis and speech/audio processing. I am particularly interested in end-to-end methods that synthesize spatial audio directly from mono mixtures without relying on source separation. 我是西南财经大学计算机科学与技术专业硕士二年级研究生。 研究方向为双耳音频合成与语音/音频处理， 尤其关注从单声道混合信号直接合成空间音频的端到端方法。
